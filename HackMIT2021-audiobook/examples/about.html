Our Mission
VoiceBook is a venture to bridge the gap between text and audio content. It brings you books and texts to audio in voice of the person you choose. It solves many socio-economic problems. Audiobooks are expensive and takes lot of time and setup to finish. With the modern world shifting to virtual technologies, the conventional textbooks are getting out of demand. Reading book is no more a regular task like it used to be. People prefer speech over texts now, there are some existing appliciations which are expensive and out of user's reach. Moreover, non-english speaking authors feel left out as they have to pay translation and publishing costs to reach a wider audience. Voicebooks overcome all the obstacles and make speech from text available for free immediately.
Our Project
This product has been especially designed for neurodivergent audience so that they can read hasslefree and hear the audio at their own pace. Voicebook target audience include the underprivileged or unemployed people as well who wants to tell their story or apply for new future opportunities.
